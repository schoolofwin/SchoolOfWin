FAQ

What does it mean that you're in beta?
It means that we're still in the starting stages so we don't have support for a lot of things yet (weekends, late-night) but stay tuned for more!
Is there a mobile application available?
Not yet, but soon! In the meanwhile, feel free to view this site on your phone or mobile device.
The dining hall was packed when you said it wasn't!
Send us an email and let us know when that was! We'll double check with our data to make sure it wasn't a bug
